Fernando Galetto 

    Ph.D. candidate

    La Trobe University, Melbourne, Australia

    f.galetto@latrobe.edu.au


 

 

 Biography: He received his B.Eng (ElecEng) degree from National Technological University, Cordoba, Argentina in 2015. And a M.Eng (ElecEng) from La Trobe University in 2019. He is currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Engineering at La Trobe University in Melbourne Australia. His primary areas of research are Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Internet of Things (IoT). His research focuses on designing intelligent systems, harnessing the power machine learning and artificial intelligence (AI) for real-time applications on Edge devices.
